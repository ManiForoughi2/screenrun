You don't get unlimited social media. You get a few short runs a day.

Runs is a minimalist screen time app built on one idea: instead of an all-or-nothing app blocker, you give yourself a fixed number of timed "runs" for each app. Each run opens one app for a set number of minutes, with a live countdown. When the timer hits zero, the app locks again. When you're out of runs, it stays locked until midnight.

No streaks to maintain. No points. No nagging. Just a hard limit you set once and then live inside.

HOW IT WORKS
- Pick the apps you want to limit (Instagram, X, TikTok, YouTube, anything).
- For each one, set how many minutes a run lasts and how many runs you get per day.
- Those apps stay blocked by default. Tap START RUN to open one for its run window.
- A Live Activity counts down on your lock screen and Dynamic Island.
- When the run ends, the app reblocks itself, even if Runs is closed.
- Everything resets at midnight.

WHY RUNS
- Turn mindless scrolling into a deliberate choice. Every run is one you decided to spend.
- Beat the doomscroll without quitting cold turkey.
- Build a calmer relationship with your phone, one run at a time.
- Less time lost to apps, more time for the things you actually meant to do.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Runs does everything on your device. No account, no sign-up, no tracking. Nothing you do is collected or sent anywhere. Your limits and usage never leave your phone.

BUILT FOR FOCUS
Black and white. Monospaced. No feed, no badges, no chrome. The app gets out of your way so you can get on with your day.

Set your runs. Spend them well.
