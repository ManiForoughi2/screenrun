A few short runs a day. That's all the social media you get.

Runs gives you a set number of timed "runs" for each app you want to limit. A run opens one app for a few minutes, with a countdown going the whole time. When it hits zero the app locks back up. Use up your runs and it stays locked until midnight, when everything starts over.

You set the numbers yourself. Three minutes a run, five runs a day for Instagram. Two minutes, three runs for X. Whatever keeps you honest.

HOW IT WORKS
- Pick the apps you want to limit. Instagram, TikTok, X, YouTube, all of them.
- Set how long a run lasts and how many you get per day, per app.
- Those apps stay locked. Tap START RUN to open one for its window.
- A Live Activity counts down on your lock screen and Dynamic Island.
- The app locks itself again when time's up, even if Runs is closed.
- Midnight resets the counters.

There's also a commitment lock if you want it. Lock your settings for a day, a week, a month, or for good. While it's locked you can make your limits stricter but you can't loosen them. Handy for the version of you at 11pm who suddenly thinks five runs wasn't enough.

FREE AND OPEN SOURCE
Runs is free. No ads, no in-app purchases, no subscription. The whole thing is open source under the MIT license, up on GitHub at github.com/ManiForoughi2/runs-screen-time. You can read exactly what it does and what it doesn't.

YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
No account, no sign-up, no tracking. Your apps, limits, and usage all live on your phone and never leave it. There's no server to send them to. I didn't build one.

Black and white, monospaced, no feed and no badges. It does the one thing and gets out of the way.

Set your runs. Spend them well.
